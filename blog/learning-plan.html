My Learning Plan

Bootcamp

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I’m hoping to get a career in the tech industry, hopefully as a web developer! I’m looking to gain experience in NZ or Aus first before going further abroad with it, but haven't yet decided where because that’s a little bit far off for the moment.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I think I will have an easy time learning from facilitators and my peers as I learn well while actively engaged and doing things, but when I learn while reading something off a page it’s more difficult. Also, even though I know it’s fine to do it, I am sometimes resistant to asking for help when I need it because I feel like the problems are too small!

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Trying to keep up with all the work and remaining focused on the task at all times will be a challenge due to long days but I really want this so I hope that will help me see it through to the end!

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Being able to communicate my ideas succinctly and efficiently with others to work on a goal, and being able to make compromises that keep everyone happy! Also being able to check in on myself from time to time to see how I’m feeling.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I hope that I will get good feedback on my work and be directed towards what I need to work on so I can use my time wisely!

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?

I expect myself to be ready and on time every day, to take time out and put in extra work when I see myself get stuck and to reach out for help when I do need assistance!

Foundations

What is my long term goal or career pathway?

Naturally, to become a Web Developer! I want to come away with the skills to be able to land a job and ultimately be able to go wherever I want in the world with the skillset that I acquire, and be able to bring projects that I envision to life.

Strengths and limitations when it comes to learning:

I am very good at not giving up on things when it becomes difficult or challenging, and I can push myself to create things that I can be proud of at the end of the day. A limitation is getting started in the first place and keeping on task when I am left to my own devices as I have ADHD and many things become a distraction to what needs to be done.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would I like to see developed in myself while at Dev Academy?

Teamwork when working with others whom I am not familiar with and learning how to negotiate and come to happy mediums that everyone agrees with when working on projects.

A commitment to how I will manage your workload in this programme:

I will wake up early and set a timer by my desk for when I will actually start to work! Even if it is some prep before class time. I need to get myself in the zone and an external signal will help me. Even if it is just a little agreement with my Google Nest. I’ll keep track of my time with Toggl as well, and take regular breaks to keep myself focused. I’ll make sure food and other chores are sorted in the evening so I won’t have to be bothered with it while I work. And I’ll make time to take a breather and walk around outside for a moment if things get muddled in my brain.

A commitment as to how and where I will seek help in a timely way:

When I realise I am slipping I know I can contact the facilitators for some help as well as the other students as I know I work much better when I am learning from another person as opposed to text off a webpage.

What I expect from the Facilitation team:

I expect them to do their best to help me when I reach out to them while I am stuck, to answer my questions when I have them, and to have supportive and constructive advice for me throughout the course.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times...?

Nope! I planned things so I wouldn’t have any kinds of commitments while the course is on. I can focus on it 100% because I know that’s what I need to do! Anything that needs attending I can do on the weekend.
